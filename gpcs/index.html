Sign up for Grandparents Clothes Service

  • Who are the Grandparents this service is helping?

    • There are currently many grandparents in the convalescent home. These grandparents are very lonely at times, and need our support. That's why once a month SSE goes to sing with the Grandparents. The grandparents currently there don't receive very new clothing, and times wear the same clothes for years.

  • What does this service plan to do?

    • This Service plans to send clothing to the Grandparents. We also aim to personalize each and every present we give them by sending personalized cards with warm messages on them.When we give the grandparents their gift, we also wish to hand them out warmly and personally.

  • Why is this Important?

    • Grandparents are vital to society. They provided love and care for years to the world. It is our duty to take care of them. In the scenario, these people were our grandparents we would put maximum effort into helping them. the grandparents at the center might not have people like you to help out. Thus, through the goodness in our hearts, we must go and help grandparents. It is also important because it makes sure our values are strong

  • How can you help?

    • Helping out these grandparents is easy. You can sign up in the google form below to help fund for a new shirt, help deliver the clothes to the center, or help with the organization of the project. If you are unable to fund the project or help in any of these way, that's okay. You can help us by giving us moral support.
